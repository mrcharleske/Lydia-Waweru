About Lydia Waweru

Preacher • Motivational Speaker • Life Coach • Humanitarian

Her Story

Born and raised in Murang’a County, Kenya, and now residing in Nairobi, Lydia Waweru’s life is a living testimony of resilience, grace, and transformation. From an early age she faced challenges that could have crushed her spirit—financial pressures, painful limitations, and difficult seasons that came in waves. She has weathered storms in marriage, persevered through afflictions, and confronted moments that seemed impossible. Yet through it all, she discovered that God’s strength is made perfect in our weakness.

Lydia is a born-again Christian who believes in Jesus Christ and the power of His resurrection. Her faith is not theoretical; it’s a daily reality that shapes how she thinks, serves, and leads. She carries a simple conviction close to her heart: being born poor is not a condemnation to live poor. Every child’s life matters, and every person can rise when given faith, love, and opportunity.

In 2017, Lydia founded Blessed Nation Ambassadors, a charity dedicated to uplifting vulnerable children, families, and communities through practical help and hope-filled programs. She later founded House Of Hope Ministry, a church community where she preaches the Gospel, mentors believers, and provides a safe place for people to grow in Christ.

Today she serves as a preacher, motivational speaker, life coach, and humanitarian—walking alongside individuals to help them heal from their past, discover their God-given purpose, and build meaningful, fruitful lives.

Scripture That Guides Her

  • Isaiah 40:31 — “Those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength…”
  • Psalm 71:14 — “But I will hope continually and will praise you yet more and more.”
