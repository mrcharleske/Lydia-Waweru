About Lydia Waweru

Lydia’s Photo

Lydia Njuguna, a compassionate and resilient woman from Murang’a, Kenya, is the visionary founder of Blessed Nation Ambassadors, a global charity organization dedicated to uplifting the less fortunate. Despite facing numerous challenges and hardships in her own life, Lydia’s indomitable spirit led her to overcome adversity. Today, she is a beacon of hope, inspiring and bringing joy to countless lives through her tireless efforts to support and empower those in need worldwide.
